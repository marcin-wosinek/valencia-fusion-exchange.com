What is fusion?

Fusion is a dance style focused on connection and interpreting the music together. We dance to a huge variety of music: sometimes music associated with a particular dance style, sometimes electronic music, sometimes hip-hop, and sometimes something entirely differentâ€”depending on the DJ.
