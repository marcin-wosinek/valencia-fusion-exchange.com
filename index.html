Valencia Fusion Exchange

12—14 april 2024

Welcome to the first Fusion Exchange in Valencia. A weekend full of dance, connection, music, flow… Where one of the most lively and dynamic fusion communities in Southern Europe will meet dancers from different places and dance communities around the world.

For people coming from away, it is a unique opportunity to enjoy the great attraction of the city of Valencia, with its climate, its people, its gastronomy, its beaches, the beauty and life of the city. As well as being able to connect with a fusion community, which prioritizes deep connection in dance, body expression and openness of heart through fusion.

For the people of the fusion community of Valencia, it'll be a great opportunity to spend whole weekend dacing with people form local community, and far away. To see other ways of dancing and connecting. To let a fresh air enter the dance and enriching themselves with it it.

We will have 3 fusion parties at night, full of magic, at the Escuela de Ruzafa. And apart from others activities such as dance picnics during the day, taster classes and some more surprises…
